      4. The wild child series by Katrina Kahler and Caz Kampbell. Review


      I have recently finished book 9 of the wild child series called Life's changing
      Its the last book of the series of Wild child. The whole series was filled with drama and friendship.
      Its amazing to see how sydney and harper become good friends from being enemies. Its full of adventure
      and its very interesting. The only thing I did not like was that Harper's very wrong actions were
      gone unpunished in book 6. Other than that, the whole series was awesome. I also liked how the author
      showed everything from different points of view. This series also had great character developement.

      Why is there no POV (point of view) of Percy Jackson in Bood of Olympus book


      Why was there no POV of Percy Jackson? This was because Percy had like an entire seies on him and percy
      was the only character which appeared in almost all books of Rick Riodan. He was taking the stage.
      Rick Riodan realized that. He replaced Percy with Jason. Many fans of Percy did not like this, but think about it-
      Jason didn't have a book at all in which he was the main character. And Percy has 5. This is the reason Jason replaced
      Percy.
